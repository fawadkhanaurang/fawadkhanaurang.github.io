About FAWAD Lab

The Future of AI with Real-World Adaptive Dynamics (FAWAD) Lab, led by Dr. Muhammad Fawad Khan, focuses on the integration of machine learning, optimization, and dynamic system modeling to address real-world challenges. The lab conducts research on human-centric robotics, neuro-mechanical control, reinforcement learning, computational fluid dynamics, and adaptive bio-systems.

FAWAD Lab collaborates with academic institutions, industry partners, and research centers across various domains including healthcare, intelligent systems, and biomechanical engineering. We are committed to open science and regularly share datasets, models, and publications on our GitHub repository.
